Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for the classic and well loved grilled cheese sandwich. The recipe is simple and begginer friendly. You can easily add more ingredients and swap out those you do not like for substitutes(e.g. white bread for brown bread).

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tablespoons of butter, divided
  • 1 slices of Chedder cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat a skillet to over meduim heat. Butter one side of a slice of bread.
  3. Place the bread butter-side down in the skillet and then put the slice of cheese on top of the bread.
  4. Butter one side of the remaining slice of bread and place the bread on top of the cheese butter side up.
  5. Cook the sandwich until it is lightly browned on one side, then flip it over and continue untill the cheese is melted
